CSIS'19

IEEE SB MESCE received the wonderful opportunity of hosting the fourth edition of The Computer Society India Symposium from the 26th to 28th of July, 2019, at MES College of Engineering, Kuttippuram, Kerala. The event themed “Integrate to differentiate” was organized by the IEEE Computer Society and co-sponsored by IEEEmadC, IEEE Student Professional Awareness Activities, Townscript. The program served as an opening for 150+ students and Young Professional participants all throughout India to associate with the best and the most dazzling talents in the sea of technological breakthroughs, and growing entrepreneurship, empowering them to unveil their potential, using the IEEE channels. The ceremonious inaugural gathering of the exciting 3 day event was accommodated in the auditorium of MESCE. The dignitaries attended to initiate the CSIS 2019 was Chief Guest Mr. Jofin Joseph, GM, India Office at Full Contact, Inc. Mr. Abilash Shivam, IEEE Computer Society Co-Chair Awards and Recognition Committee MGAB, Dr A.S Varadarajan, the Principal of MES College of Engineering, Dr. Sasidharan Sreedharan, HOD of Computer Science Department of MESCE and Chapter Advisor for IEEE Computer Society, Dr. Maya G, Branch Counsellor of IEEE MESCE SB.
