frizbee'19

IEEE SB MESCE hosted the fourth edition of its trademark annual flagship event FrizBee, on the 20th, 21st and 22nd of September 2019, with a bunch of over the benchmark speakers and workshops. FrizBee’19 had a schedule consisting of top notch speakers and five interesting workshops. FrizBee’19 commenced with the inaugural ceremony which started at 2:30 PM. The registrations were open from 12:30 PM for the convenience of the delegates. The ceremony began with a silent prayer followed by Code of Ethics of IEEE. Mr. Nadheer PP, the Chairman of IEEE SB MESCE delivered the welcome address and expressed his greetings to everyone on and off the dais. Dr.Maya G, the Branch Counselor of IEEE SB MESCE, gave the presidential address and expressed her heartfelt appreciation to the SB for its hard work in conducting the event. Followed by this, Dr. A S Varadarajan, the Principal of MESCE addressed the audience. He was indeed proud about the Student Branch for successfully hosting the event, for the fourth time. Dr. Ramlatha Marimuthu, Former Global WIE Chair, delivered the inaugural address and inaugurated the event. She concluded by wishing FrizBee’19 to be a grant success like the previous ones. Bringing a close to the ceremony, Ms. Pooja Vineeth, the Secretary of IEEE SB MESCE, proposed the vote of thanks expressing her gratitude to all the dignitaries and everyone who worked tirelessly for the event to become a reality. The event came to a close with the valedictory session at which the speakers were presented with mementos and delegates were asked to give their opinions and feedbacks on FrizBee’19. FrizBee’19 winded up with a good note of unleashing things beyond expectation for the next sequel of the successful event
